Felix is a simple game to play, using only 3 keys: The right key, left key and space bar. When the right key is pressed, Felix is moved towards the right side of the screen. Likewise, when the left key is pressed, he is moved towards the left side of the screen. When the space bar is pressed Felix jumps. Since the game takes place in an underwater environment, he has the ability to jump multiple times in a row, giving the illusion that he is swimming through the ocean!
