Getting a high score


In order to be immortalized inside of the high scores page, you must be able to complete the given level in a short a time as possible. High scores are determined by the shortest completion time for a level, and not anything else.  


If you finish a level, the game will automatically determine whether or not you are worthy of a high score. If you are, you will be greeted by a screen where you can proceed to enter your name, and thereby secure your immortality (at least until another Joe comes along and beats your time).


Finally got a high score!


After you write in your name, you will be taken back to the word search for level selection.
